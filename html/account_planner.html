Executive Summary Aliaxis is a global leader in advanced fluid management solutions, enabling access to water and energy through its comprehensive portfolio of sustainable and innovative solutions for building infrastructure, industrial, and agricultural applications. The company's purpose is to address the world's water challenges and facilitate the transition to clean energy, embodying this through a strategy focused on sustainability, innovation, being locally grounded, and future-focused.

In 2022, Aliaxis reported significant growth with revenues of €4.3 billion, a notable increase in net profit to €371 million, and an emphasis on reducing its carbon footprint and increasing the use of recycled materials in its products. Despite facing challenges such as geopolitical turmoil and inflation, Aliaxis has maintained a strong financial position and continues to invest in expanding its operations, particularly in the United States, and in enhancing its product development capabilities.

The company prioritizes customer intimacy, operational excellence, sustainability, and the development of its workforce to drive its strategic objectives and address pressing environmental challenges.
